# EmoVoice-DB

**Summary**: EmoVoice-DB is a 40.45-hour synthetic English emotional speech dataset with natural-language emotion descriptions. It is designed to train fine-grained emotion-controllable TTS models.

**Sources**:
- Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-06

---

## Overview

EmoVoice-DB contains 22,100 emotionally expressive English speech samples totaling 40.45 hours (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf). It covers angry, happy, sad, surprised, fearful, disgusted, and neutral emotion categories with natural-language emotion descriptions (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf).

## Construction

The dataset construction has three stages: generate text and emotional descriptions with GPT-4o, synthesize emotional speech with GPT-4o-audio, and filter samples using WER-based post-processing (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf). The speech generation uses five distinct speaker voices to diversify timbre and speaking style (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf).

## Use in EmoVoice

[[emovoice]] uses EmoVoice-DB for emotional fine-tuning after standard TTS pretraining (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf). The authors also augment the dataset by rewriting each emotion description twice with GPT-4o, which reduces WER from 3.83 to 2.73 in their EmoVoice experiment (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf).

## Caution

EmoVoice-DB is valuable for scaling [[freestyle-emotion-prompting]], but it is still [[synthetic-emotional-speech-data]] generated by a strong teacher model (source: Yang et al. - 2025 - EmoVoice LLM-based Emotional Text-To-Speech Model with Freestyle Text Prompting.pdf). The dataset may reflect GPT-4o-audio's emotion rendering style rather than the full distribution of acted or spontaneous human emotional speech; this limitation needs verification from external evaluation.
